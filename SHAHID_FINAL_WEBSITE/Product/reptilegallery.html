    Reptile Gallery


    Banana Ball Python

    (Python Regius)

    This snake is infamous for its bold yellow-coloured blotches and dark freckles, but what makes mine so unique is that it is an albino. You will not find those red eyes anywhere and anytime soon!

    Pinstripe Cornsnake

    (Pantherophis Guttatus)

    This garden snake is known for its stunning pattern, but mine is especially gorgeous. This juvenile has a rare tan stripe running through the middle with a charming brownish-red surrounding it.

    Hermann's Tortoise

    (Testudo hermanni)

    This adorable tortoise shines with its abnormally bright shell. The glowing green and soft brown colours make this creature unique and beautiful. Furthermore, its abnormally thick scales make it stand out even more.

    Gargoyle Gecko

    (Rhacodactylus Auriculatus)

    This frightening yet sweet gecko has dragon-like eyes that are piercing and haunting. This reptile's nearly magical body and dinosaur-like limbs are what make it intriguing and impressive.

    Viper Gecko

    (Teratolepis Fasciata)

    These reptiles are fierce, as they are tiny. They have a sharp and vicious appearance, with piercing and terrifying eyes. Their glares are intense and haunting, which is what makes this creature so mesmerizing.

    Asian Water Monitor

    (Varanus salvator)

    Although this juvenile may seem soft and friendly, it can eventually grow up to be a monstrous five feet long with an insatiable hunger. It is, in my opinion, the best pet lizard anyone can have!

    About

    I am a frog connoisseur, collector and breeder. That being said, I am also interested in reptiles such as snakes, as they are unique and fascinating creatures. My love for these creatures began when I was young, and my passion only grew stronger as I aged. One time, I had nearly two-hundred frogs croaking and thirty reptiles crawling at the same time in my house.
