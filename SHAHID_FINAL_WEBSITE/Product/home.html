    Love Frogs ?

    If you are a frog lover, then you have come to the right place. My name is Moosa Shahid and welcome to my website dedicated to my frogs and reptiles. To put it simply, I love frogs, as they animate my world with excitement and wonder. My love for them all started when I was a child, as their strange manners and unique looks captivated me to the extent that I decided to own a few. Eventually, my small collection turned into a life-long passion, and if you want to see what I have accomplished so far, check out my gallery!

    About

    I am a frog connoisseur, collector and breeder. That being said, I am also interested in reptiles such as snakes, as they are unique and fascinating creatures. My love for these creatures began when I was young, and my passion only grew stronger as I aged. One time, I had nearly two-hundred frogs croaking and thirty reptiles crawling at the same time in my house.
