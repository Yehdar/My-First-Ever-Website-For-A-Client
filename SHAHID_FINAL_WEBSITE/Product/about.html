    Who Am I ?

    Moosa Shahid

    Frog & Reptile Enthusiast

    My frogs and reptiles are an integral part of my life to the extent it defines who I am as a person. These creatures filled my childhood with curiosity, my boyhood with passion and now, my adulthood with satisfaction. Furthermore, I take immense pride in my creatures because they made me a kind, caring and ambitious individual. Finally, I am grateful to be a father of four, a manager at my workplace and an expert in zoology. If you want to learn more about me, send me a message through Instagram or my contact page!

    About

    I am a frog connoisseur, collector and breeder. That being said, I am also interested in reptiles such as snakes, as they are unique and fascinating creatures. My love for these creatures began when I was young, and my passion only grew stronger as I aged. One time, I had nearly two-hundred frogs croaking and thirty reptiles crawling at the same time in my house.
