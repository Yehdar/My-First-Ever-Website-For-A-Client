    Blue Poison-Dart Frog

    (Dendrobates Azureus)

    This frog has a gorgeous dark-blue pattern that paints a picture of a black ocean during a storm. Its beauty is mesmerizing, as it oozes a feeling of a bright blue night.

    Harlequin Frog

    (Atelopus Varius)

    This amphibian has a luxurious pattern painted all over its body. Its black blobs with hints of orange on the bright green canvas are what make it so captivating. Also, it is one of the world's rarest frogs.

    About

    I am a frog connoisseur, collector and breeder. That being said, I am also interested in reptiles such as snakes, as they are unique and fascinating creatures. My love for these creatures began when I was young, and my passion only grew stronger as I aged. One time, I had nearly two-hundred frogs croaking and thirty reptiles crawling at the same time in my house.
